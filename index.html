Callie’s Commission Terms of Service

By commissioning me, you are agreeing to the terms of service below.

Payments

  • All commissions must be paid full price upfront before I can start.
  • An adult must approve of the payment if you are under 18.

Process

  • Contact via Discord, Twitter or Instagram to begin talking about commission details.
  • Screenshots may be taken during the conversation about commissions.
  • Work in progress screenshots will be offered, unless declined.
  • Will only make revisions three times.

Refunds/Cancelling

  • Callie has the right to cancel a commission at any point during the commission process.
  • Refunds depend on the situation.
  • If you are no longer interested in the commission, you will be issued a half-refund if it has already been started.

Things to expect

Clients should expect Callie to:

  • Ask permission to post on social media such as, but not limited to: Instagram, Twitter, Discord, Deviantart.
  • Ask permission to stream the process of the commission through Twitch.
